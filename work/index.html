Vacuum cleaner

Vorwerk Kobold is one of Vorwerk’s oldest-established divisions and one of the company’s best-known segments. When Vorwerk invented the original Kobold upright vacuum cleaner in 1929, it succeeded in building powerful suction into a very small space. The next new departure soon came along, when Vorwerk became the first company to bring direct sales to Europe. Driven by innovations, today’s Kobold product line encompasses not only the upright and canister-type vacuum cleaners, but also a robot vacuum and a window cleaner.
